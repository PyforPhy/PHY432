Arunima K. Singh is an associate professor in the Department of Physics at Arizona State University (ASU) and a graduate faculty member of the Materials Science and Engineering Department at ASU. Her research focuses on accelerating materials discovery, synthesis, and application using first-principles computations and data science. She is particularly interested in physical phenomena occurring at surfaces and interfaces of materials. 

She received her doctorate in 2014 from Cornell University. Prior to joining the ASU faculty, Dr. Singh was a postdoctoral associate at the Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory and at the National Institute of Standards and Technology. Dr. Singh is a recipient of the Department of Energy (DOE) Early Career Research Program Award and is a Thrust co-lead at ULTRA, a DOE-Energy Frontier Research Center.
